§ 01
Profile

Who I
Am

Computer Science & Engineering undergraduate at Visvesvaraya Technological University, Belagavi — building at the intersection of machine learning, signal processing, and real scientific data.

My work spans deep learning for high-energy astrophysics, RLHF safety, reinforcement learning policy optimization, and AI-driven environmental modeling. I've secured ₹18L+ in research grants and presented at international conferences across India and the Middle East.

Beyond the lab — state-level debater, football player, founder of the research collective Aliens on Earth (AoE), and sponsored delegate at the Linux Foundation's Open Source Summit 2025.

Projects

Featured
Projects

01
Satellite Data Analysis for Transient Detection

Built a lightweight pipeline to analyze AstroSat SXT Level-2 light curve data. Developed an anomaly detection script to flag significant flux deviations and solar flare interference in X-ray time-series. Presented at ISRO's AstroSat 2026 conference.

PythonAstropyFITSISRO
02
Regret-Aware Policy Optimization (RAPO)

Architected a novel RL framework solving the credit assignment problem in environments with large temporal gaps. Implemented environment-level memory modules to suppress harmful trajectory replays in RLHF pipelines.

PyTorchRLRLHFICML 2026

EcoWave — AI Traffic Management System

AI-driven traffic management system formally appreciated by DCP N. Niranjan Raj Urs, Belagavi City. Utilized computer vision, facial recognition, and V2X communication to reduce urban congestion and air pollution.

Computer VisionOpenCVV2X
