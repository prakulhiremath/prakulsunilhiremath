Featured
Projects

06
01
Satellite Data Analysis for Transient Detection

Built a lightweight pipeline to analyze AstroSat SXT Level-2 light curve data. Developed an anomaly detection script to flag significant flux deviations and solar flare interference in X-ray time-series. Presented at ISRO's AstroSat 2026 conference.

Python Astropy NumPy FITS ISRO
02
Regret-Aware Policy Optimization (RAPO)

Architected a novel RL framework solving the credit assignment problem in environments with large temporal gaps between actions and rewards. Implemented environment-level memory modules to suppress harmful trajectory replays in RLHF pipelines.

PyTorch RL RLHF AI Safety ICML 2026
03
EcoWave — AI Traffic Management System

AI-driven traffic management system formally appreciated by DCP N. Niranjan Raj Urs, Belagavi City. Utilized computer vision, facial recognition, and V2X communication to reduce urban congestion and air pollution.

Computer Vision OpenCV V2X Civic Tech
