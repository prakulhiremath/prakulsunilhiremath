Featured
Projects

01 Satellite Data Analysis for Transient Detection Space
Built a lightweight pipeline to analyze AstroSat SXT Level-2 light curve data. Developed an anomaly detection script to flag significant flux deviations and solar flare interference in X-ray time-series. Presented at ISRO's AstroSat 2026 conference — proceedings page 146.
PythonAstropyNumPyFITSISRO
02 Regret-Aware Policy Optimization (RAPO) Mind
Architected a novel RL framework solving the credit assignment problem in environments with large temporal gaps between actions and rewards. Environment-level memory modules suppress harmful trajectory replays in RLHF pipelines. Submitted to ICML 2026.
PyTorchRLRLHFAI SafetyICML 2026
03 EcoWave — AI Traffic Management System Space
AI-driven traffic management system formally appreciated by DCP N. Niranjan Raj Urs, Belagavi City. Utilized computer vision, facial recognition, and V2X communication to reduce urban congestion and air pollution in real deployment.
Computer VisionOpenCVV2XCivic Tech
04 BIOLOOP & URJAWAVE (NAIN 2.0) Earth
Dual-project initiative backed by a ₹3L government grant from NAIN 2.0 (Govt. of Karnataka), hosted at IIIT Dharwad. BIOLOOP targets carbon capture through AI-monitored algae reactors. URJAWAVE develops clean energy solutions using AI-integrated approaches.
